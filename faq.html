FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Are PEIMI resin boards food safe?

They are designed for serving dry and prepared foods such as cheese, fruit, bread and appetizers. Avoid cutting directly on resin areas.

Can I customize color or logo?

Yes. Custom resin colors, engraving, logo marks and packaging are available for personal, wholesale and corporate orders.

Do you offer wholesale?

Yes. PEIMI supports gift shops, home decor stores, kitchenware retailers, hotels, event planners and corporate gift buyers.

Are all boards identical?

No. Every board has unique wood grain and hand-poured resin patterns.
