Care Guide

How to care for your PEIMI resin board.

Designed for serving & display

PEIMI resin boards are designed for cheese, fruit, bread, appetizers, desserts and decorative table styling. For cutting, use the natural wood side and avoid direct knife contact with the resin artwork.

Care tips

  • Hand wash only with mild soap and warm water
  • Dry immediately with a soft cloth
  • Do not soak in water
  • Do not place in dishwasher or microwave
  • Apply food-safe mineral oil to wood when needed
